---
title: The name
---

The name Daedalus is deliberate. In Greek mythology, Daedalus embodied ingenuity and craftsmanship. He designed the Labyrinth — a masterwork of complexity that confined the formidable Minotaur. His wings symbolise the human drive to overcome our limits, a pursuit filled with both triumph and tragedy, a delicate balance between ambition and hubris. It seemed a fitting name for coaching that aims to channel that creative spirit to support individuals and teams through complex challenges and unlock their true potential.
