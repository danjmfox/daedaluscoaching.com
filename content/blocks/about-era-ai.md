The current edge of my work is the intersection of coaching and AI. I build custom agents, automation systems, and decision-support tools — and I work with leaders and coaches on what it means to work well with AI rather than around it.

It is, in some ways, the same question I've been asking since I first encountered agile: what does it take to work well in conditions of genuine complexity, where the right answer isn't knowable in advance?
