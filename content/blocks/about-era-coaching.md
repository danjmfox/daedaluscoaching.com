A decade of coaching work across organisations: team coaching, executive coaching, large-scale facilitation for fifty or more people, designing coaching capabilities at organisational scale. I worked with product teams, digital agencies, energy companies, healthcare groups, and financial services. Different sectors, different problems, the same underlying pattern.

The most important thing I learned in that period is that the presenting issue is rarely where the real work is. The team that's stuck is often expressing a structural condition. The leader who feels isolated is often carrying something that belongs to the whole system. Getting good at reading that — and finding where the real leverage is — is what the work became.
