I work across a range of engagement types depending on what the situation calls for. For organisations, that might be a time-limited contract or a longer-term embedded arrangement — including full-time contract and permanent roles for the right context. For individuals and teams, engagements are typically standalone: a defined coaching programme, a series of facilitation sessions, or a single-day intervention. If you're not sure what fits, [get in touch](/contact) and we can work it out together.
