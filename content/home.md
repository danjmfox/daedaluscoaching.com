---
title: Daedalus Coaching
description: Coaching for leaders navigating complexity — individually and with their teams
---

Leadership is complex. The systems you work in resist simple answers, and the pressure to produce clarity from ambiguity is constant. Most of the difficulty isn't technical. It's relational, structural, and political — and it rarely resolves through frameworks alone.

I work at the intersection of strategic thinking and human dynamics. With individuals, I create the conditions for deeper reflection — the kind that unlocks a different quality of decision. With teams, I work in the room, helping groups move from individual expertise toward genuine collaboration. With organisations, I focus on the structural and cultural conditions that either release or suppress the capability already present in the people doing the work.

The people I work with are typically senior — leading significant functions, navigating complex stakeholder landscapes, or carrying the weight of change initiatives that matter. What they share is a desire to think more clearly about the systems they're in, and to act more deliberately within them.

I've worked across software delivery, architecture, programme management, and organisational design. That breadth shapes how I work now: I'm less interested in applying a single model and more interested in helping you find what's actually true about your situation.
