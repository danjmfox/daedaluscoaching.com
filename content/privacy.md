## Who we are

Daedalus Coaching Ltd is a coaching and organisational development practice based in the United Kingdom. This policy explains how we collect and use personal data when you visit this website or contact us.

## Data we collect

**Contact enquiries:** When you submit the contact form, we collect your name, email address, and the content of your message. We use this information solely to respond to your enquiry.

**Website analytics:** This site does not use any analytics tools. No tracking scripts, cookies, or behavioural data are collected when you visit.

## Lawful basis

We process contact form data on the basis of **legitimate interest** — specifically, to respond to enquiries from people who have chosen to contact us.

## How long we keep data

Contact enquiries are retained for as long as needed to manage the enquiry and any resulting engagement, after which they are deleted.

## Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.
