---

## Wave: DISCOVER / [REF] Persona ID

**Primary**: Recruiter / Hiring Manager — arrives via CV or LinkedIn profile, validates candidate credibility before progressing.

**Secondary**: Evaluating Leader (coaching prospect) — arrives via warm referral from a trusted peer. See `persona-prospective-client.yaml`.

---

## Wave: DISCOVER / [REF] Opportunity Statement

The site's dominant job is professional portfolio validation for recruiters and hiring managers, not direct coaching client acquisition. Credential signals and transformation evidence are the highest-ROI content investments; content must serve a 10-second validation scan before serving the coaching prospect.

---

## Wave: DISCOVER / [REF] Validated Assumptions

- **LinkedIn → CV → Site is the primary arrival sequence** (confidence: high) — recruiters encounter the LinkedIn profile or CV first; the site is a validation step, not a discovery mechanism.
- **Warm referral is the dominant route for coaching enquiries specifically** (confidence: high) — when coaching conversations happen, they begin with a referral, not cold search.
- **Recruiters scan for credential badges before reading prose** (confidence: high) — pattern-matching on ICF, PSM, SAFe, etc. happens in the first 10 seconds; prose is secondary.
- **Organic/Google search is not a meaningful channel** (confidence: medium) — no evidence of coaching clients finding via search; recruiter search flows through LinkedIn and job boards.

---

## Wave: DISCOVER / [REF] Invalidated Assumptions

- **The site is primarily a coaching practice storefront** — invalidated. The economic model is permie/contract Agile Coach employment with professional coaching supplementary. The site serves recruitment validation first.
- **Warm referral is the dominant *overall* arrival channel** — invalidated for the full picture. Warm referral dominates coaching enquiries; overall arrival is dominated by recruiter/hiring-manager via CV or LinkedIn.

---

## Wave: DISCOVER / [REF] Dropped Options

- **Service sub-pages as near-term build** — rejected. Recruiters don't need service breakdowns; coaching prospects arrive via referral and use the contact form. Low ROI for dominant persona.
- **Blog / thought leadership as primary acquisition mechanism** — remains deferred (DEF-1, STR-2). No evidence it drives recruiter or coaching-client conversion. Revisit with proof-of-practice framing if STR-2 progresses.

## Wave: DISCOVER / [REF] Constraints Established

- **No analytics** (PL-1 DONE) — conversion data unavailable; all decisions based on owner self-knowledge and sector norms. Baseline to establish post-launch.
- **Site is a validation step, not a discovery mechanism** — for both personas, the site is encountered after initial contact (CV/LinkedIn or referral). Content must serve validation speed, not attraction.
- **Dual-purpose site** — must serve the recruiter's 10-second credential scan and the coaching prospect's 60-second approach evaluation without one undermining the other. These are compatible if credential signals are above the fold and approach narrative is reachable but not forced.
- **Coaching services are supplementary** — content must not oversell a pure-play coaching practice that doesn't match the economic reality. Case studies should reflect org transformation work (Agile Coach context) not individual coaching only.
